Fortress Guardians: Orc Siege

How to Play

Welcome to the defense! Your mission is to protect your base from the onslaught of orcs determined to reach the other side of the board. Use your wits and resources to block their path and eliminate them before they can breach your defenses.

Building Structures

  • Constructing any structure costs 50 coins. Plan your defenses wisely.
  • To build, select the desired structure from your inventory and place it on the board where strategic.

Battling Orcs

  • Each orc you defeat earns you 25 coins. Use these coins to bolster your defenses.
  • Stay vigilant! Orcs will come in waves and attempt different paths to breach your base.

Winning & Losing

  • Your base has a heart count. Each orc that reaches the end of the board will cost you one heart.
  • The game is over if your heart count reaches 0. Protect your base at all costs!

Strategize, defend, and adapt to the challenges ahead. Good luck!
